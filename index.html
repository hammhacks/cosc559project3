Asteroids Game Help Guide

Objective

Your mission is to destroy all asteroids while avoiding collisions and enemy fire. Large asteroids break into smaller ones until they are completely eliminated. A UFO may appear and attack you with burst fire, adding to the challenge.

Controls

Movement

  • ↑Thrust forward
  • ←Rotate left
  • →Rotate right

Shooting

  • Space Fire bullets to destroy asteroids and the UFO

Scoring

  • Destroying large asteroids: 100 points
  • Destroying medium asteroids: 50 points
  • Destroying small asteroids: 25 points
  • Destroying UFO: 500 points
  • Surviving a UFO attack: 20 points based on evasion

Dangers & Health

  • Colliding with an asteroid: Instant game over
  • UFO bursts: Each hit deals 10% health damage
  • Direct UFO hit: 50% health damage
  • Game over when health reaches 0%

Tips for Survival

  • Stay mobile! Constant movement helps avoid asteroids and UFO attacks.
  • Use controlled thrusts to maneuver strategically.
  • Take out larger asteroids early to control debris.
  • When a UFO appears, focus on dodging first, then attack.

Good luck, pilot!
